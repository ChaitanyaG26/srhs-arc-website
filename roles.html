TEAM STRUCTURE

Roles

Excluding club officers, who oversee all teams from a local perspective, every team role falls into one of three categories below. Pilots and copilots are secondary roles — anyone in those seats still holds one of the three primary roles.

Our Official Mascot, Jim Yang
WP‑01Team Roles
Flying Falcons, 23/24
EN

Engineers

Tasked with construction — either building the initial drone or attaching the mechanisms designed by the CAD drafters, ideally under their supervision.

PR

Programmers

Handle the autonomous missions required for competition — from managing the drone's flight path over waypoints to remotely triggering mechanisms with precision at each checkpoint.

Prototyped Drone and Cube Mechanism
CAD

CAD Drafters

Focused on the mission-specific mechanism design — from a claw built to pick up a tennis ball, to a triangular cutout shaped to hold a cube.
