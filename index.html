WP‑01Our Mission

Empower yourself with robotics

Our school club is designed to give real working experience — especially in the numerous STEM industries — to everyone. Whether it's strengthening college applications, learning how robotics applies to aeronautics, or preparing for competition, ARC aims to improve some part of every member's journey.

01

Hands-On Learning

Participate in club activities to gain hands-on experience building and programming drones for submission at the official ARC competition.

02

Innovation & Creativity

We encourage innovation and creativity through design challenges and internal competitions, which also factor into how roles and teams are chosen.

03

Mentorship & Guidance

Our team of experienced mentors provides guidance and support to help you excel in present and future ARC projects.

04

Community Engagement

Join a community of like-minded members, share ideas, and collaborate on exciting robotics initiatives together.
